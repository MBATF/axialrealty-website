Who We're Looking For

Every Agent at a Different Stage — All Welcome

We don't have a single mold. Whether you're brand new, seasoned, or building a side business, there's a place for you at Axial.

New & Newer Agents

Just got your license or in your first couple of years? We'll walk alongside you — teaching contracts, negotiation, client communication, and how to build a sustainable business from day one.

Experienced Agents

Already closing deals and looking for a better fit? We offer a collaborative environment, direct broker access, and no corporate red tape — just focused support so you can do more of what you're good at.

Full-Time & Part-Time

Building real estate alongside another career or family commitment? We respect your schedule and help you maximize the time you have. Real estate on your terms.

What You Get

More Than a Brokerage — A Platform to Build On

We've built the infrastructure so you can focus on serving clients and closing deals.

1-on-1 Broker Mentorship

Direct access to David Tung — a licensed Texas broker with 10+ years of DFW experience. Get real answers to real questions, not a ticket system.

Contract & Transaction Training

We review contracts together, walk through every step of a transaction, and make sure you're confident presenting offers and protecting your clients.

Marketing & Brand Support

Leverage the Axial Realty brand with professional marketing materials, a presence on axialrealty.com, and support for your personal branding efforts.

Collaborative Team Culture

We're a small, focused team — not a factory. Wins are celebrated, questions are welcomed, and no one is left to figure it out alone.

Flexible Schedule

Work when and how it fits your life. We judge success by results, not hours logged at a desk. Full-time drive or part-time hustle — both work here.

Technology & Systems

Access to modern real estate tools, transaction management, and the MLS. We keep the admin overhead low so you spend more time with clients.
