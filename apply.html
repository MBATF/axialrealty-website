Authorization & Acknowledgment

Section I — Authorization and Acknowledgment (TXR-2003)


Each applicant authorizes Landlord, Landlord's agent, and their representatives to: (i) verify all information in this application including, but not limited to, obtaining consumer credit reports, criminal history reports, rental history, and employment and income verifications; (ii) contact any person or entity listed in this application; and (iii) retain this application for future reference, whether or not applicant is approved.


Each applicant certifies that the information in this application is true and correct and acknowledges that any misrepresentation or omission of material information in this application may be grounds for rejection of the application, immediate termination of any resulting lease agreement, or both.


Applicant understands that submission of this application does not guarantee approval or create a binding agreement to lease. Approval is subject to verification of the information provided herein, completion of background and credit screening, and execution of a final lease agreement.


In the event applicant is approved, applicant agrees to execute a Residential Lease Agreement in the form prescribed by the Texas REALTORS® or another form acceptable to Landlord, and to pay all required deposits and fees upon execution of such lease.


Applicant acknowledges receipt of, and has read, all pages of this application, including any addenda. Applicant acknowledges that Landlord's agent represents Landlord and not Applicant, unless a separate written agreement otherwise provides.
