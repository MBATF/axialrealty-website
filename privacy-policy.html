Effective Date: April 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 26, 2026

Axial Realty LLC ("Axial Realty," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website axialrealty.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and property address when you submit a form (CMA request, contact, lease application, or property owner inquiry).
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, and referring URLs collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookies — small data files stored on your device to improve your experience. See our Cookie Policy for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Prepare Comparative Market Analyses (CMAs) and property evaluations
  • Process lease applications in compliance with Texas law
  • Send relevant real estate information you have requested
  • Improve our website and marketing
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not rent or trade your personal information either. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — third-party tools we use to operate our website and business (e.g., form processors, email services, DocuSign for e-signatures).
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, court order, or Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC) regulations.
  • Professional advisors — attorneys or accountants under confidentiality obligations.

4. Texas-Specific Disclosures

Axial Realty LLC is a licensed real estate brokerage in the State of Texas. We comply with all applicable Texas real estate laws, including requirements of the Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC). Our Information About Brokerage Services (IABS) form is available on our website as required by Texas law.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve site performance and analyze traffic. You may manage cookie preferences via our cookie banner or your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Lease application data is retained in accordance with Texas landlord-tenant laws.
